Our Location

The location for Shenanigans is 40 West 14th Street Helena, MT. This location is situated directly next to the Silver Star Steak Company and less than one mile from Carroll College.

This location is within walking distance from several residential areas and Carroll College's campus.

Directions

From Carroll College: drive staight on Getchell St. Take a left on W 14th St. The destination will be on your left.

From Safeway: take a right out of Safeway onto Montana Ave. Continue straight onto E Lyndale St. Take a left onto Getchell St, a right onto 40 W 14th St. The destination will be on the left.
